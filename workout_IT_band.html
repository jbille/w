Weekly Workout Plan
Knee · IT Band Syndrome · Lower Back
ITBS Focus: Emphasis on single-leg control and reduced TFL dominance. If an exercise causes next-day hip/TFL soreness, reduce volume or remove it.
Weekly Rules Important
  • No high-volume lateral band work before runs.
  • Strength should not cause lingering soreness.
  • Avoid aggressive IT band rolling near the knee.
