Weekly Knee Tendinitis Plan (with Lower Back Additions)

DayActivityDetails
Sunday Run
Monday Run + Glutes & VMO (Knee) + Lower Back & Calves Warm-Up (knee-friendly):
  • Glute Bridges – 2×10
  • Leg Swings (front/back) – 10/side
  • Lateral Band Walks – 2×10 each way
Knee Strength:
  • Single-Leg Toe Touch – 3×8/side
  • Band Side-Stepping – 3×10 each way
  • Standing Hip Adduction (ankle band pull straight out) – 3×12/leg
  • Terminal Knee Extensions (TKE) – 3×15/leg
  • Spanish Squat – 3×10 (2-sec hold)
Lower Back (added):
  • Banded Pull-Through – 3×10–12 (hinge; glutes/hamstrings/lumbar)
Accessory (added):
  • Standing Calf Raises – 3×12–15
Cool-Down:
  • Quad + hamstring stretch – 2×30s each
  • Foam roll quads & inner thigh – 2–3 min
Tuesday Rest
Wednesday Run
Thursday Adductors & Hamstrings (Knee) + Lower Back Warm-Up:
  • Leg Swings (front/back & side/side) – 10 each direction
  • Mini Band Lateral Walk – 1–2×10 each way
  • Glute Bridges – 2×10
Knee Strength:
  • Copenhagen Side Plank (modified) – 2×10–15s/side
  • Romanian Deadlift – 3×10 (light DB/KB ok)
  • Eccentric Hamstring Curl (stability ball) – 3×8
  • Standing Hip Adduction (soccer kick) – 3×10/leg
  • Single-Leg Glute Bridge – 2×10/leg
Lower Back (added):
  • Stability Ball Back Extension – 3×10–12 (control; neutral neck)
Cool-Down:
  • Adductor + hamstring stretch – 2×30s each
  • Foam roll inner thigh & hamstrings – 2–3 min
Friday Run
Saturday Glute Medius & Balance (Knee) + Lower Back Warm-Up:
  • Mini Band Lateral Walk – 2×10 each way
  • Side Leg Swings – 10/side
  • Glute Bridges – 2×10
Knee Strength:
  • Clamshells (with band) – 3×15/side
  • Single-Leg RDL – 3×8/leg
  • Star Balance Reach – 2×5/leg
  • Side Plank with Top-Leg Raise – 2×10/side
Lower Back (added):
  • Kettlebell Good Morning – 3×10 (light; hinge pattern)
Cool-Down:
  • Piriformis + lateral hip stretch – 2×30s/side
  • Foam roll glutes & IT band – 2–3 min
